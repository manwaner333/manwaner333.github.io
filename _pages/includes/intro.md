My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and mathematics. I leverage mathematical theory to improve the trustworthiness and interpretability of AI models, with a particular focus on large language models, including hallucination detection, privacy, and safety. In parallel, I explore the use of AI for scientific discovery, including identifying dynamical systems from observational data.
Currently, my research group focuses on the following directions:

- LLM-based Agentic AI Systems: planning, reasoning, continual learning, and safety
- Trustworthy LLMs: hallucination detection, safety, and privacy
- AI for Mathematics and Scientific Computing
